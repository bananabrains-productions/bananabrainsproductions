Who is Bananabrains Productions?

Bananabrains Productions is a corporation run by frantic monkeys and apes that act in a hive-mind state and usually refer to their collective in the first person. BBP creates animations, fan animations, original music, comics, doodles, art, blah blah blah blah blah. BBP mostly runs its operations through Youtube (at the moment!). Eventually, BBP plans on producing at least one animated series, one comic series, and two albums.
